Understand your spreadsheet before the AI touches it.

Shortcut Audit analyzes Excel files entirely in the browser. It maps every formula dependency, flags hardcoded values, detects inconsistencies, and assigns a health score, giving analysts full visibility into model integrity before automating with Shortcut.
